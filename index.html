Fingerprinting

First, you'll need to schedule an appointment to get your fingerprints taken, as this is a requirement to work with Boston Public Schools.

Once you've had your fingerprints taken, you'll need the receipt from that you receive for the next step.

Had your fingerprinting appointment, and have your receipt? Click below to start filling out forms!

Forms

Before you begin, make sure you have all of the documents listed to the right. You will need to add images of them to the forms.

  • Fingerprinting receipt
  • A voided check
  • Identification, such as a passport or a driver's license and a Social Security card. Click for details.

  • Fingerprinting receipt upload (page 1)
  • CORI/SORI Authorization form (page 2)
  • Form I-9, Employment Eligibility Verification (pages 3-12)
  • Form W-4, Federal Tax Withholding (pages 13-14)
  • Form M-4, Massachusetts Tax Withholding (page 15)
  • Massachusetts Deferred Compensation SMART Plan, OBRA (pages 16-17)
  • Social Security statement (pages 18-19)
  • Health Insurance Information (page 20)
  • Direct Deposit (pages 21-22)
  • Emergency Contact form (page 23)
  • Technology Policy and Guidelines (pages 24-28)

All forms that are required by Boston Public Schools have been collected into one PDF document. The individual forms are listed to the left.

Once you fill in all required fields and submit, your information will be saved in a document on PDF filler on a secure server. Learn more about the security of your information here.

Please read through all of the forms carefully, as your digital signature is legally binding.

Please note that you will not be able to save a partially completed document to come back to later.
